What is Emoji Salad?

Emoji Salad is an emoji party!

No download needed - play with your friends for free via SMS.

Like Pictionary, the name of the game is to dominate your friends by guessing the movie, book, or song typed out in emojis. Unlike Pictionary, a plucky bot named Emojibot is on hand to feed you new phrases, enforce the rules, and help you if you get stuck.
